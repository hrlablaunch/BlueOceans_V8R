About BlueOcean

Blue Ocean Middle East Environmental Services LLC, we are committed to delivering top-tier environmental services that contribute to a sustainable future. Established in 2017, we have quickly become a leading certified Waste Oil Management Company, recognized by the Environmental Departments of Dubai and Sharjah.

Our core expertise lies in the collection, treatment, and recycling of waste oil, slop oil, and sludge oil. We operate with the highest standards of quality and safety, managing all aspects of our operations in-house to ensure complete control and customer satisfaction.

We pride ourselves on our dedicated team, state-of-the-art equipment, and a strong commitment to environmental protection. Our mission is to safeguard natural resources and habitats by continuously seeking innovative solutions for waste management. At Blue Ocean, we are passionate about creating a cleaner, safer environment for current and future generations.
