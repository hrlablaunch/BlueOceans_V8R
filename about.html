Waste Oil, Slop Oil, and Oily Sludge Collection, Transportation & Disposal Process

Blue Ocean Middle East Environmental Services LLC has a fleet of 6 road tankers, one with a capacity of 10000 gallons, two with a capacity of 5000 gallons, and three with a capacity of 1200 gallons. The vehicles are fitted with diesel-driven pumps to enable recovery from tanks and pits. The tankers are manned by trained staff that have extensive experience in the used oil collection business. Blue Ocean Middle East Environmental Services LLC drivers collect all kinds of waste oil adhering to stringent rules, procedures, and regulations to ensure the safe and responsible collection and transportation of this waste. Waste oil collected is brought to the company processing plant where it is offloaded into holding tanks for recycling. We are working in this field professionally with a sound track record and offering 24-hour services. All our vehicles are in perfect state and properly equipped for safety and emergency incidents, and we have the latest equipment capable of handling recycling and proper final disposal of waste liquid and hazardous waste.
